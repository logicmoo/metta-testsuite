Elon Musk is a South African-born entrepreneur and business magnate known for his work in revolutionizing multiple industries, including electric vehicles, space exploration, renewable energy, and telecommunications. He was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa.
Musk is the CEO and lead designer of SpaceX, a private aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company that aims to reduce space transportation costs and enable the colonization of Mars. SpaceX has achieved numerous milestones, including the development of the Falcon 1, Falcon 9, and Falcon Heavy rockets, as well as the Dragon spacecraft, which supplies the International Space Station (ISS).
In addition to SpaceX, Musk is the CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc., an electric vehicle and clean energy company. Under his leadership, Tesla has popularized electric cars with models like the Roadster, Model S, Model X, Model 3, and Model Y, and has advanced the adoption of sustainable energy solutions through products like solar panels and home batteries.
